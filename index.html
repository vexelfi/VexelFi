— Our story

A startup with a big idea

VexelFi was born in early 2024 out of a simple frustration: crypto payments are still too hard for regular people and small businesses. We're a lean founding team building the tools to change that.

We're pre-revenue, moving fast, and looking for talented people who want to shape a product from scratch — not maintain someone else's legacy code.

— What we're building

Our Products

01

Crypto Payment Widget

A plug-and-play checkout widget for any website. Accept crypto in under 10 minutes of integration.

02

Yield Strategies (Beta)

Simple DeFi yield tools for non-technical users. Deposit, earn, withdraw — no PhD in Solidity required.

03

Developer API

REST + WebSocket API for crypto infrastructure. Currently in closed beta with early corporate clients.

04

VexelFi Wallet

A mobile-first, non-custodial wallet focused on usability. Coming Q3 2025.

05

On-Ramp & Off-Ramp

Local payment methods → crypto and back. Targeting underbanked markets first.

06

B2B Dashboard

A self-serve analytics and payout dashboard for businesses using our payment rails.
